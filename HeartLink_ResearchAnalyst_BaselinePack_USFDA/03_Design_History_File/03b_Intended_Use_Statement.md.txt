# Intended Use Statement — HeartLink (Wellness)

**Intended User:** Adults (≥18 years) managing heart-failure–related symptoms.  
**Environment of Use:** Personal mobile device (iOS/Android).  
**Purpose:** To record and visualize self-reported symptoms and weight changes, and to provide educational trend summaries that encourage user awareness and communication with a healthcare provider.  

HeartLink does **not** provide diagnostic, prognostic, or treatment guidance.  
It is not intended for use in emergencies.

# Future Intended Use (HeartLink Clinical)
An FDA-cleared version may analyze patient-reported data and connected-device vitals to flag patients who may require provider review. It would not independently modify therapy or replace clinical judgment.
