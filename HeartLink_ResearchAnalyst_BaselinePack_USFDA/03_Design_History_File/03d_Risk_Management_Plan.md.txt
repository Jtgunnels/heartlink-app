# Risk Management Plan — per ISO 14971

## Objective
To identify and control potential hazards associated with HeartLink’s use and algorithmic outputs.

## Process Summary
1. Identify foreseeable hazards (use errors, data entry errors, misinterpretation).
2. Estimate severity and probability.
3. Define risk controls (UI design, labeling, warnings).
4. Verify risk control effectiveness.

## Roles
- **Risk Owner:** Joshua Gunnels, PA-C  
- **Reviewer:** Regulatory QA Reviewer (TBD)

## Risk Acceptability Criteria
Low or Medium risks acceptable with implemented control; High requires redesign.
