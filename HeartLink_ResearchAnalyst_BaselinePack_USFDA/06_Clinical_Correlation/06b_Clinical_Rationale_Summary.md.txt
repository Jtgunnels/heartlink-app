# Clinical Rationale Summary

Each symptom tracked by HeartLink aligns with evidence-based markers of fluid retention and functional decline in heart failure.

| Symptom | Clinical Reference | Rationale |
|----------|-------------------|------------|
| Weight gain | AHA/ACC/HFSA 2022 § 7.3 | Indicates possible fluid overload. |
| Edema | AHA/ACC/HFSA 2022 § 7.2 | Correlates with volume status. |
| Shortness of breath | AHA/ACC/HFSA 2022 § 6.1 | Reflects pulmonary congestion. |
| Fatigue | AHA/ACC/HFSA 2022 § 6.2 | Reflects cardiac output and NYHA class progression. |

Outputs are phrased to encourage user awareness and provider discussion, consistent with educational device language.
