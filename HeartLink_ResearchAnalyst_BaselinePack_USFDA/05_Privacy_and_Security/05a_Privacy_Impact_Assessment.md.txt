# Privacy Impact Assessment

HeartLink (Wellness) stores all user input locally on the device.  
No personal health information (PHI) is transmitted or retained on external servers.

**Safeguards**
- Local data encryption at rest.
- No third-party analytics.
- User consent presented during onboarding.

Future clinical versions will implement HIPAA-compliant backend with Business Associate Agreements (BAAs).
